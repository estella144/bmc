About Us

We are a fish and chips takeaway restaurant in Binley, Coventry.

Binley Mega Chippy became famous after videos were posted about it on TikTok, with a slideshow showing pictures of it, and someone singing "Binley Mega Chippy" in the background. It later became viral.

The history isn't that deep or rich. Things go viral all the time, because they do.
